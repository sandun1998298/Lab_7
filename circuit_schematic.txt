COMPONENT PLACEMENT ON BREADBOARD:
- Arduino Uno: Straddles center gap (pins in rows 6-19)
- LCD: Right side (rows 10-25)
- Push Button: Left side (rows 5-6)
- LED: Left side (row 8) with resistor
- Piezo Buzzer: Left side (rows 20-21)
- Resistors: In tie point rows as needed

POWER RAIL CONNECTIONS:
- Top red rail (+): Connected to Arduino 5V
- Top blue rail (-): Connected to Arduino GND
- Bottom rails: Connect to top rails with jumper wires

STEP-BY-STEP WIRING GUIDE:
=========================

1. POWER CONNECTIONS:
   - Red jumper: Arduino 5V → Breadboard + rail
   - Black jumper: Arduino GND → Breadboard - rail

2. LCD CONNECTIONS (6 wires):
   - Arduino Pin 12 → LCD RS (pin 4)
   - Arduino Pin 11 → LCD E (pin 6)
   - Arduino Pin 5 → LCD D4 (pin 11)
   - Arduino Pin 4 → LCD D5 (pin 12)
   - Arduino Pin 3 → LCD D6 (pin 13)
   - Arduino Pin 2 → LCD D7 (pin 14)
   - LCD VSS (pin 1) → - rail
   - LCD VDD (pin 2) → + rail
   - LCD V0 (pin 3) → - rail (or center of 10kΩ pot)
   - LCD A (pin 15) → + rail
   - LCD K (pin 16) → - rail

3. LED CIRCUIT:
   - Arduino Pin 13 → LED anode (long leg)
   - LED cathode (short leg) → 220Ω resistor → - rail

4. BUZZER CONNECTIONS:
   - Arduino Pin 8 → Buzzer + (marked side)
   - Buzzer - → - rail

5. BUTTON CIRCUIT:
   - Arduino Pin 7 → one button terminal
   - Same terminal → 10kΩ resistor → + rail
   - Other button terminal → - rail

TESTING CHECKLIST:
================
□ Power: 5V between + and - rails
□ LCD: "Morse Terminal" appears on power-up
□ LED: Lights when Pin 13 goes HIGH
□ Buzzer: Sounds when Pin 8 activated
□ Button: Pin 7 reads LOW when pressed
□ Serial: 9600 baud communication works

TROUBLESHOOTING:
===============
- No LCD display: Check power and contrast (V0)
- Scrambled LCD: Verify data pin connections
- No button response: Check pull-up resistor
- No sound: Verify buzzer polarity
- No LED: Check resistor and LED orientation
